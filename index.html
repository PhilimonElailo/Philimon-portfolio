About Me

I am a web developer and trainer with experience in technical instruction, learner support, and audience engagement. My work combines hands-on web development with communication skills built through training and customer-facing roles.

At Learning Lions, I teach web development and mentor learners as they build practical digital skills. Before that, I worked in sales at SunKing and M-KOPA for three years, where I learned how to understand user needs, explain products clearly, and support adoption at the community level.

I am especially interested in opportunities where I can help shape curriculum, produce engaging learning content, and use storytelling to make technology education more relevant and accessible.
